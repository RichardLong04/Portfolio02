My Story..

Hi, my name is Rich. I'm an aspiring front end web developer. I recently finished the Web Design and App Development program at Illinois Tech in Chicago. I must say that the two biggest things I learned there were first how to be open to learning new and better ways of doing things and second how much I still have to learn and grow to become a web developer. I also have a degree in Multimedia from Portland Community College, so I can create and produce content for the web also. What I love most about creating websites other than solving problems, is that what I’m doing has the potential to touch the lives of a good chunk of the world’s population. I hope someday that what I’ll be able to use my skills to work for organizations that are trying to make the world a better place.
